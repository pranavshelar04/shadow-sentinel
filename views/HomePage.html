About the Project

Shadow Sentinel is an advanced security system designed to detect and prevent Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks in real-time. Leveraging machine learning models, we analyze network traffic patterns to distinguish between legitimate and malicious requests, ensuring robust protection for businesses and organizations.
